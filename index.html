ABOUT

Bridging technical complexity and human-centered clarity

Hi, I’m Anvita Kisara! I’m a UX/UI Designer dedicated to bridging the gap between human behavior and digital design. I love the process of design to development. Whether it's sketching wireframes or thinking through the logic behind a system, I relish every step of the creative process. My journey into UX has been marked by a commitment to understanding not just how something works, but why it should work that way for the people using it. I majored in Computer Science and Engineering during my undergrad, where I learned to think systematically about problems. Through my projects, I gained hands-on experience translating technical constraints into real-world solutions, building a solid foundation in engineering and problem-solving. Building on that foundation, I've been developing my skills in user-centered design: conducting user research, building journey maps and personas, and creating prototypes that meet end-users' specific needs. My technical background gave me a strong sense of functionality; this shift has pushed me to lead with empathy and center the human experience in every design decision. With this combined foundation in engineering and design, I'm excited to bring that same care to work that genuinely improves people's lives by designing experiences that make wellness and health information more accessible, intuitive, and human-centered.
